Porcini

The Porcini is a popular mushroom amoung foragers because it is edible to be cook.
They tend to grow near areas with a lot of vegetation. If the mushroom underside looks discolored they may contain maggots
No matter what you find it is good to be careful when searching.

They are Worldwide

Genus Region Season
Boletus edulis Forest June - October
