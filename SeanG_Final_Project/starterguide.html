Starter Guide for
Mushroom Foraging

This guide will help you understand
the purpose of mushroom foraging.

Disclaimer: Wild Mushroom can be dangerous to one's health and
should be inspected by a professional before consuming it.
Also try to join a mushroom club that specilizes in this hobby like the Mycological Association

Mushroom foraging is a common pastime
that is done by all age groups who curious about Fungi in the wild.

Typically a group will drive to a location that has a lot of trees and shrubs
These areas are typically shaded so it can be diffiuclt to find.

    Tips

    -Join a mushroom forager club

    -Bring a Basket and something to cut the mushrooms with

    -Apply Bug Spray

    -Clothing that has no holes

    -Bring Water

    -Rain Poncho incase it rains
